About

The Journey from Engineering to Product Management

I didn't start in product management. I started with circuits, embedded systems, and hardware logic — and that's exactly what makes me different.

The Arc

Engineering → Operations → AI → Product

🔧 Engineering Foundations

ECE at NSUT Delhi. Circuits, embedded systems, VLSI, RTL. Learned to think in systems, constraints, and trade-offs.

🏭 Industrial Automation

MECON Limited (PSU, Govt. of India). SCADA telemetry, plant automation, 20+ KPIs, Power BI dashboards. Learned how enterprise operations actually work.

🤖 AI Platform Building

EdgePlay AI, VoiceEval. Built 0-to-1 cloud pipelines, annotation infrastructure, data ops. Learned to ship AI data products.

📋 Product Management

PRDs, RICE prioritization, user discovery, North Star metrics, AARRR funnels. Learned to think, communicate, and decide like a PM.

Philosophy

How I Think About Products

User Pain > Feature Creep

Prioritize solving acute operational bottlenecks before expanding scope. The best features are the ones that eliminate friction.

Data-Informed, Not Data-Obsessed

Use telemetry to validate hypotheses, but rely on empathy and user research for 0-to-1 product direction.

Speed to Value

Minimize time-to-value by automating repetitive steps and reducing manual workflows wherever possible.

Platform Scalability

Build reusable pipelines and schemas rather than isolated scripts. The infrastructure is the product.
