The Problem

HR Teams Notice Attrition Too Late

Employee turnover costs organizations 50% to 200% of an employee's annual salary. HR leadership typically relies on exit interviews — a reactive approach that captures feedback after the employee has already decided to leave.

No systematic early-warning mechanism existed to identify high-risk employee cohorts before departure decisions were finalized.

Key Discovery 39.8% Sales Rep Attrition
Methodology

Feature Engineering & Risk Segmentation

Engineered 5 key telemetry features from 1,470 raw employee records. Wrote 11 targeted SQL queries to segment high-risk cohorts across department, tenure, overtime status, and salary band.

  • Sales Representatives exhibit 39.8% attrition rate — highest across all job roles
  • Employees in the 0-2 years tenure band show 3x higher departure risk compared to 5+ year tenure cohort
  • Overtime workers have significantly elevated attrition probability across all departments
  • Low salary band combined with zero stock options strongly correlates with early voluntary departure
