The Problem

Broadcasters Need Real-Time Match Insights

Cricket analysts and media teams require rapid statistical breakdowns during live match broadcasts and post-match coverage. Compiling multi-season player performance, venue dynamics, and toss trends manually from raw ball-by-ball logs is too slow to meet broadcast deadlines.

No automated product existed to aggregate 17 seasons of telemetry and instantly output web dashboards, print PDFs, and spreadsheet reports.

Key Finding

The Toss Advantage Myth

Common cricket wisdom asserts that winning the toss provides a decisive match advantage. Analysis of 1,090 matches revealed toss-winning teams win only 50.8% of matches — statistically indistinguishable from a random coin toss.

Platform Connection

Relationship to EdgePlay AI

EdgePlay AI is the data creation layer (annotating video footage for computer vision). IPL Analytics is the data consumption layer (analyzing structured telemetry for end users). Together, they demonstrate full lifecycle data product management.
