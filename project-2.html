01 / Problem Statement

Manual Speech Evaluation Doesn't Scale

Educational institutions, recruiters, and training organizations evaluate spoken communication skills manually. This creates severe operational bottlenecks: high costs, delayed feedback, and significant evaluator bias.

Instead of asking "How can we run speech recognition algorithms?", VoiceEval asks: "How can the entire speech evaluation experience become faster, fairer, and more actionable for users?"

Product Type AI Voice & Assessment Platform
Target Users Students, Job Applicants & Recruiters
Key Focus Fluency, Pronunciation & Pace Scoring
Outcome Instant Objective Feedback Loop
02 / User Discovery

Target Personas & User Needs

P1
Job Candidates / Students

Goal: Receive rapid, non-judgmental, actionable feedback on fluency, speech clarity, and pace to prepare for interviews.

Pain Point: Waiting weeks for human evaluations with vague feedback like "needs improvement".

P2
Recruiters & Evaluators

Goal: Screen thousands of applicants efficiently with zero evaluator fatigue or subjective scoring variance.

Pain Point: Spending hundreds of hours manually interviewing baseline candidates.

03 / Product Strategy

User Journey & System Architecture

The core product insight is that candidates and recruiters do not care about speech model parameters. They care about receiving fast, clear, and actionable feedback.
