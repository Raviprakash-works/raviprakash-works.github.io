PRD-001 EdgePlay.ai — Modular API Backend & Automated Pipeline

1. Product Scope: Defined schema and worker execution pipelines for a modular FastAPI backend spanning 9 core entities: Projects, Users, Assets, Tasks, Jobs, Workers, Assignments, Events & Storage.

2. Target User Persona: Data Annotators & CV Engineers requiring frame-accurate pre-trimmed, version-controlled clips.

3. RICE Score: 8.4 / 10 — Reach: 100% team, Impact: High (50% turnaround reduction), Confidence: 90%, Effort: 4-week sprint.

4. Functional Requirements: Auto-detect frame rates & transcode to 1080p/60fps. Slice into 10-second event clips at delivery timestamps. Generate Backblaze B2 pre-signed URLs with 24hr TTL. Embedded Label Studio layer for annotation task sync.

5. Success Metric: Reduce dataset preparation time from 4 hours to under 10 minutes per match.
