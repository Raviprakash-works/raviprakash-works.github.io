01 / Problem Statement

Turnover Costs 50–200% of Annual Salary

Replacing an employee is extremely costly once recruitment, onboarding, and lost productivity are factored in. Most HR teams fail to notice attrition risk factors until exit interviews take place.

This project transforms raw HR data into a proactive retention telemetry product, highlighting high-risk employee cohorts (OverTime + Low Job Satisfaction + Early Tenure) so HR leadership can intervene before employees resign.

Dataset 1,470 Records · 35 Features
Stack Python · SQL · Power BI
Target Persona HR Directors & Department Leads
