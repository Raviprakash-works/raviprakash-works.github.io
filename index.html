04 / Product Thinking

Frameworks, PRDs & Product Philosophy

💡 4 Core Product Principles
  • User Pain > Feature Creep: Prioritize solving acute operational bottlenecks before expanding product scope.
  • Data-Informed, Not Data-Obsessed: Use telemetry to validate hypotheses, but rely on empathy and systems thinking for 0-to-1 direction.
  • Speed to Value: Minimize time-to-value by automating repetitive steps in human workflows.
  • Platform Scalability: Build reusable data pipelines and metadata schemas rather than isolated one-off utilities.
🎯 Decision & Prioritization Frameworks

Structured methodologies I apply to evaluate trade-offs and align technical roadmaps:

📄 Sample Product Requirements Document (PRD) Snippet
PRD-001 EdgePlay AI — Automated Video Annotation Queue & Backblaze B2 Sync

1. Problem Statement: ML engineers manually trimmed 50GB+ raw match files, creating 4-hour operational delays before annotators could start labeling.

2. User Persona: Data Annotators & Computer Vision Engineers requiring standardized frame rates and pre-trimmed clips.

3. Feature Requirement: Cloud ingest trigger via FFmpeg segmentation to slice video into 10-second event clips, auto-generating Backblaze B2 S3 pre-signed URLs and populating the Label Studio annotation queue.

4. Success Metric: Reduce annotation task preparation time from 4 hours to < 10 minutes (50% overall turnaround reduction).
