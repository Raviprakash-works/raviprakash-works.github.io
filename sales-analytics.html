The Problem

Unmonitored Discounts Destroying Margins

The sales organization lacked clear policy guardrails on promotional discounting. Account executives routinely offered 40%+ discounts to close deals, assuming top-line volume would offset margin erosion.

In reality, orders with 40%+ discounts were consistently margin-negative, resulting in -$99,559 in cumulative revenue leakage that remained invisible to executive leadership until this diagnostic analysis.

Product Type Commercial Strategy
Users Sales VPs & CFOs
Stack Python · SQL · Power BI
Key Output 20% Pricing Guardrail
Key Insight

The Discount-Profit Cliff

Strategic Recommendation

Cap automated sales discounts at 20% maximum across all product categories. Any discount exceeding 20% requires formal VP approval and a written margin justification, preventing profit erosion while retaining deal flexibility for strategic enterprise accounts.
