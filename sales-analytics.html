The Problem

Unmonitored Discounts Destroying Margins

The sales organization lacked clear policy guardrails on promotional discounting. Account executives routinely offered 40%+ discounts to close deals, assuming top-line volume would offset margin erosion.

Analyzed 5,009 orders across 4 years via Python EDA & 12 SQL queries; built a 3-page drill-through Power BI dashboard and margin-waterfall analysis showing 40%+ discounts drive a net loss of -$9,559 vs $29,988 profit at zero discount.
