01 / Commercial Insight

High Discounts Destroyed Profit Margin

Sales teams frequently grant aggressive discounts to drive order volume without monitoring the net profit impact. Analysis of 5,009 transactions revealed that orders discounted by 40% or more accumulated -$99,559 in net losses.

By establishing automated pricing guardrails and discount telemetry dashboards, executive leadership was empowered to cap discounts at 20%, preserving margins while maintaining top-line revenue growth.
