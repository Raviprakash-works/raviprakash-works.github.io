The Problem

Manual Speech Evaluation Doesn't Scale

Traditional spoken language evaluation relies on human evaluators — an expensive, slow, and inherently biased process. Assessing 100 candidates manually takes days, and scores fluctuate depending on evaluator fatigue and background.

Candidates receive zero real-time diagnostic feedback to improve, while recruiting teams struggle to screen spoken communication skills at scale.
